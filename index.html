ERIK STEWART-SMITH

Erik is currently studying design at Eastern Washington University. This spring, he will complete the program with a Bachelor of Design, and a Web and UX Design Certificate. Before he went back to school, he did 20 years in the Air force in network and systems administration. Erik gained valuable experience solving complex systemic problems as a Cyber Operations Analyst. He is confident his background and training at EWU puts him in a good position to pursue a new career in UX and UI Design.

A lifestyle by design

For Erik, design is a lifestyle, not just a new career motivation. From everyday hobbies like arts, crafts, model making, and photography to playing guitar and music production, he’s able to apply core UX/UI practices to enhance those processes thanks to his training at EWU. And, his background in information systems technology coupled with diverse learning experiences in the US and abroad -- from growing up inside the arts and entertainment centers of Northern and Southern California to 11+ years in Europe and Asia -- Erik’s ultimate goal is to become a well-rounded, culturally diversified, full-stack front-end designer who’s philosophy is simple: the user’s and client’s needs and expectations are always at the top of the stack!
